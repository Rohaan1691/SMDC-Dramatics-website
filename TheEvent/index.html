About The Event

About The Event

Annual Dramatica is an opportunity for us to flaunt our talents on stage.From serious plays to comedic plays to dance performances,each has a lot of hard work put into it and each is a contender for the best performance award of the year.The judges include our Society-Heads,our Presidents and a chief guest who is normally associated with drama and theatre in our country.The event is around 4hrs long with a well made stage,a proper sound system and of course an enthusiastic audience. Hope you become part of this audience in our upcoming event.

Vission

Shakespeare was absolutely right when he said that life is a stage and we are all merely actors, playing our parts throughout different stages of life and here we are, a very fine example of drama to its full potential. With a touch of sarcasm, absurdity, love, hate, pain, magic and humor. Here at SMDC's Dramatics Club you will find every theme very beautifully captured by our young and talented cast members. Our actors put their very souls into every characters and our scripts are always going to be a pure piece of art, that will leave you in awe. We promise to revive theatre. So, lights, camera and action!
